// Additions \\

- (certain) Grenades now increase temperature.

- Added a debug mode so I can ask you for information easily. It's a config setting.
- Finalized the Stasis-Starter item, a new item that lets you put someone in stasis (cheaply) by admitting it and freezing patient. (Side effects included)
- Added a Cryo Tank, lowers temperature when used! (Also highly dangerous!)
- Players with Husk Symbiosis now gain a speed increase when cold, or a speed decrease when hot.
- Certain damage types have a chance to explode a 3rd Degree Frostbitten Limb. (Like a grenade or gunshot). Imagine Jason X style.
